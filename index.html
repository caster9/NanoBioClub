NanoBio Club

Exploring the Intersection of Nanotechnology and Biotechnology

Club Mission Statement

The mission of the NanoBio Club is to advance scientific knowledge and innovation in nanotechnology and biotechnology, promoting sustainability, ethical research, and interdisciplinary collaboration to solve global challenges.

Club Objectives

  • Develop a Prototype for a Biodegradable Plastic
  • Engineer a Microbial Solution for Wastewater Treatment
  • Launch a CRISPR-Based Plant Enhancement Project

Club Goals

  • Promote Innovation
  • Foster Collaboration
  • Provide Career Development
